About Me

I'm a dedicated software developer with a passion for creating high-quality mobile and web applications. With extensive experience in both iOS and Android development, as well as full-stack web solutions, I bring a comprehensive approach to every project.

I'm the founder and lead developer at Wave Of Solution Technology (wOs), based in Islamabad, Pakistan. As a freelancer on Upwork, I've had the privilege of working with clients from around the world, delivering solutions that exceed expectations. My focus is on building reliable, scalable applications that not only meet technical requirements but also provide exceptional user experiences.

What sets me apart is my commitment to clear communication, timely delivery, and ongoing support. I believe in building long-term relationships with clients by solving their problems effectively and maintaining their applications for continued success.
